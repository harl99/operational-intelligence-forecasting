# Operational Intelligence Forecasting Platform

Professional Time Series Forecasting and Operational Intelligence project focused on industrial energy consumption forecasting, operational analytics, and temporal machine learning.

---

# Project Overview

This project simulates a realistic industrial operational environment including:

- hourly energy consumption
- production throughput
- ambient temperature
- maintenance events
- anomaly events
- dynamic operational behavior
- temporal forecasting patterns

The project demonstrates professional forecasting workflows and operational intelligence analysis for industrial systems.

---

# Business Objective

Forecast industrial energy consumption and operational costs while analyzing temporal operational behavior and system dynamics.

---
